"Out of clutter, find simplicity. In the middle of difficulty, opportunity."

Our events are open to EGRMS 6th graders whose families have committed to delaying smartphones and social media.

It's not about saying no to technology forever — it's about saying yes to childhood, friendship, and being fully present, just a little longer.

How it works.

The simple shape of every gathering

01

Phone-free, always

Every event is for kids who don't have smartphones or use social media. No exceptions, no FOMO.

02

Low-cost, all-in

Ticket prices cover everything — food, drinks, the activity. Venmo Karin to RSVP and you're done.

03

Parent-organized

This is a grassroots effort by EGR families, not an official school program. Just neighbors helping neighbors.

04

Spread the word

Know an EGRMS 6th grader who fits the bill? Share an invite. The more, the merrier.
