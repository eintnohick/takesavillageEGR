A pledge by EGR families

It takes a village EGR.

We're a community of East Grand Rapids parents standing together to delay smartphones and social media for our kids — because growing up shouldn't happen on a screen.

For all EGR kids Parent-led Stronger together
The Pledge

We pledge to

Delay a smartphone until 9th grade, and social media until age 16.

*Watches and flip phones / dumb phones are A-OK.

Why this matters.

The simple why behind the village

01

For all EGR kids

This isn't a single grade or school program — it's a community-wide commitment for any East Grand Rapids family who wants to delay phones and social media.

02

Stronger together

"My kid is the only one without a phone" doesn't have to be true here. The more families who pledge, the easier it gets for every kid in EGR.

03

Yes to childhood

It's not about saying no to technology forever — it's about saying yes to friendship, presence, and being a kid for just a little longer.

04

Parent-organized

A grassroots effort by EGR families, not an official school program. Just neighbors helping neighbors do the hard, good thing.
